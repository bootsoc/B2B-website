Our Story

PrecisionB2B was founded on the belief that successful marketing begins with clarity. After years of working with technology companies, we saw a gap between generic lead providers and the hyper‑targeted campaigns needed by modern B2B buyers. We set out to build a studio that combines strategic insight with cutting‑edge martech to deliver results at every stage of the funnel.

Today, our team spans marketers, data scientists and creative storytellers across India, the UAE and the US. We’re committed to transparency, compliance and continuous learning — values that help us build lasting partnerships with our clients.
